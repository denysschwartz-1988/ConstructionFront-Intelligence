North American LNG delivery capacity is becoming a live market issue.

Fluor's JGC Fluor BC LNG II joint venture has received a limited notice to proceed for the proposed Phase 2 expansion of LNG Canada's export facility in Kitimat, British Columbia, supporting early planning ahead of a potential 2026 Final Investment Decision.

The milestone adds to a crowded North American LNG project pipeline, with major activity also progressing at Sabine Pass, Commonwealth LNG, Woodside Louisiana LNG, CP2 LNG and Cedar LNG.
